Product Specifications

The device features an aluminum build with smooth metal finish that screams premium quality, and the display even opens using one finger. In the MateBook X Pro, you’ll also find two USB Type-C with one that includes support for Thunderbolt 3, one USB Type-A, which you can use to connect legacy accessories without having to use a dongle. Furthermore, Huawei includes an adapter with VGA, HDMI, and an additional USB port.

And the power button has built-in fingerprint scanner that makes signing in to Windows 10 a really fast experience. Huawei’s latest laptop is supper thin and light, it’s just under 15mm thick and weighs close to three pounds. When you open the device, you’ll get a 13.9-inch touch-enabled display with the thinnest bezels you’ll find on any laptop in its category. Huawei says that it has a 91 percent of screen-to-body ratio. The display on the MateBook X Pro is also high-resolution with great color representation. In addition, the 3:2 aspect ratio will help to improve productivity when working with documents or surfing the internet.
